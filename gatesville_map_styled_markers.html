Deep Dive 1: The Developing Bones of a Utility Map for Gatesville, TX
Now with markers, overlays and objects!

This map shows some of the large utility features in the city of Gatesville, including water tanks, water mains, and treatment facilites. I'm also experimenting with dislaying smaller features such as customer meters, water mains repairs, and reported leaks.

The map is centered on the geographic middle of Gatesville because the majority of the utility assets lie within the area displayed with that geographic center at a zoom level of 15.
