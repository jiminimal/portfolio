Deep Dive 1: The Developing Bones of a Utility Map for Gatesville, TX
Now with markers, overlays and objects!

This map shows some of the utility features in the city of Gatesville, including water mains, selected customer meters, water mains repair clamps, and reported leaks, as well as a general interests town center marker.

The leaks have detailed information avaiable in an info box. The town center marker has both a rollover box and an info box, and the other features have minimal identifying information, such as meter number, or pipe type and diameter.

The map is centered on the geographic middle of Gatesville because the majority of the utility assets lie within the area displayed with that geographic center at a zoom level of 15.
